Operations

Receive. Manage. Kit. Fulfill.

Four operating disciplines, each with documented procedures and traceable outputs. Hours of operation 7 AM–4 PM CST.

01 · Receiving & AuditInbound material handling+
Verification against packing lists; condition assessment; WMS onboarding; verification of Boeing transfer status (Released / QC Hold / Return Pending / Awaiting Disposition); chain-of-custody confirmation; segregated processing for held or restricted material.
02 · Inventory ManagementIn-facility control+
Location control, FIFO or customer-specified strategies, daily cycle counts (A/B/C categories), annual physical, discrepancy reporting. Adjustments ≥ $5K require Boeing pre-approval.
03 · Kitting & AssemblyKit build process+
Pick to work order, assemble per spec, QA audit, package and stage. Process returned kits per Boeing-directed disposition. Maintain return transaction history and personnel handling records.
04 · Order FulfillmentPickup & release+
Email-received orders processed through Envision's WMS-integrated workflow. Completed orders staged in the designated Boeing pickup zone under defined custody-transfer controls. Standard SLA: orders by 11 AM CST stage 9 AM CST next business day; after 11 AM CST, second business day. After-hours pickup model in development with dedicated east-side access point for controlled 24/7 retrieval.
Material Disposition Authority

Envision will not independently release Boeing-held material. Boeing quality authority governs disposition; Envision controls physical segregation and downstream handling. Tied to Readiness Hub →
